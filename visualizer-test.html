How to use in your project:
1. Copy lap-visualizer.js and lap-visualizer.css to your project
2. Include them in your HTML: <link rel="stylesheet" href="lap-visualizer.css"> and <script src="lap-visualizer.js"></script>
3. Create a container: <div id="lap-visualizer-container"></div>
4. Initialize: new LapVisualizer({containerId: 'lap-visualizer-container', data: results}).render()
